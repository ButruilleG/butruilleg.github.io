4xSynth

Depending on your point of view, 4xSynth is either one eight oscillator synthesiser, or two four-oscillator synthesisers.

Each tab functions as its own independant monosynth, so they can either be layered while playing the same note, or assigned different ranges so multiple notes can be played simultaneously.

4xSynth is available for free in 32 bit and 64 bit as a VST, VST3, and standalone executable for Windows systems.

Follow @4xSynth for version updates and news on 4xSynth
